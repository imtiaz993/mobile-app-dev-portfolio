Munitec Online

The app is intended for authorities and regional councils to manage the schools' transportation system. It allows parents to track the location of their children on the transportation and provide parents with peace of mind.

Technology Stack: Flutter

Features:

  • RTL Support (Hebrew)
  • Firebase Authentication anonymous sign-in
  • API integration
  • Firebase Realtime Database
  • Background Location Fetching
  • Push Notifications
